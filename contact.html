Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to develop a custom software solution?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple web application might take 6-8 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions can take 3-6 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your development process?

We follow an agile development methodology with six key phases: Discovery, Design, Development, Testing, Deployment, and Support. We keep you involved throughout the process with regular updates and feedback sessions.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages including bug fixes, security updates, feature enhancements, and 24/7 technical support to ensure your software runs smoothly.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in integrating new solutions with existing systems, databases, and third-party APIs. We'll assess your current infrastructure and design seamless integrations.

What industries do you serve?

We serve multiple industries including Real Estate, Education, Healthcare, Retail & E-commerce, and Startups & SMEs. Our team has deep domain expertise across these sectors.

How do you ensure project quality?

We maintain high quality through code reviews, comprehensive testing (unit, integration, and user acceptance testing), security audits, and adherence to industry best practices and coding standards.
